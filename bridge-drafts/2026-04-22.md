stretch isn't a property, it's a behavior. every fabric has 3 directions: warp (stiffest), weft (looser), and bias (45°, most stretch). a "12% stretch" spec usually means warp. weft might be 4%. bias could be 30%+. if you design assuming uniform stretch, your prototype looks right and your production run warps at the seams.

Stretch isn't a property, it's a behavior.

Every fabric has 3 directions: warp (stiffest), weft (looser), and bias (45°, most stretch).

A "12% stretch" spec usually means warp. Weft might be 4%. Bias could be 30%+.

If you design assuming uniform stretch, your prototype looks right and your production run warps at the seams.
