## LinkedIn version

hot take: stretch isn't a material property. it's a behavior.

the same fabric can have wildly different stretch characteristics depending on how you cut it (bias vs grain), how the seam is oriented, and what it's laminated or attached to.

I've had renders look perfect and then the sample arrives and the stretch panel goes the wrong direction entirely. because the render doesn't know about seam construction.

"this fabric stretches 40%" means almost nothing without the full construction spec. material data sheets are the beginning, not the end.
