## LinkedIn Post

Every softgoods CAD model is wrong by 10-15mm. That's seam allowance - the fabric tucked inside every stitch.

CAD shows the finished shape. Production needs the cut shape, which is bigger.

Most designers learn this when their first prototype comes back too small.
