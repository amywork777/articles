## LinkedIn Post

AI will not replace CAD designers.

It will replace designers who don't understand manufacturing.

The designers who survive will be the ones who can look at an AI-generated design and immediately spot:
- The draft angle that won't release from the mold
- The wall thickness that will warp
- The undercut that requires a side action
- The tolerance stack that guarantees assembly failure

AI can generate geometry. It can't yet feel the resistance of a bad design in your gut.

That intuition is the moat.
