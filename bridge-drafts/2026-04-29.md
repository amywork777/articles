## LinkedIn Post

The gap between a beautiful render and a working part is 200 emails with the factory.

Each email is a compromise you didn't plan for:
- "This material is 16 week lead time, can we use this instead?"
- "Your perfect radius needs to be 2mm because that's what the endmill can do"
- "The texture you spec'd requires a $40k mold treatment"
- "This tolerance is possible but triples the unit cost"

The render is the easy part. The emails are the job.
