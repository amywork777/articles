## LinkedIn Post

I wrote about why adhesives are harder than they look.

Screws and snaps fail predictably. Adhesives fail in ways that surprise you - temperature swings, humidity, wrong surface energy, incomplete cure. Most designers pick an adhesive from a catalog and hope for the best.

The article covers what I learned the hard way: bondline thickness matters, surface prep is 90% of the work, and "structural adhesive" doesn't mean it works on everything.
