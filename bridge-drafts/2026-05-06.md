# May 6, 2026 - Bondline Thickness

**Category:** MFG_HOT_TAKE

## X Post (~280 char)

bondline thickness: the spec nobody puts on drawings. it's the gap between two parts filled with adhesive. too thin and the bond starves. too thick and it shrinks and warps. epoxy likes 0.1-0.25mm. acrylic likes 0.05-0.1mm. most cad models show parts touching. production has to invent the gap. if you care about the bond, dimension it.
