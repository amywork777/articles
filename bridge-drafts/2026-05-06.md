Bondline thickness: the spec nobody puts on drawings.

It's the gap between two parts filled with adhesive. Too thin and the bond starves. Too thick and it shrinks during cure.

Most structural adhesives want something like 0.1-0.2mm. But CAD models show parts touching. Production has to guess.
