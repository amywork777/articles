## LinkedIn Post

Dyneema is 15x stronger than steel by weight. Used in body armor, yacht rigging, cut-resistant gloves.

But it won't take dye (you're stuck with white or gray), melts around 150°C, and creeps under sustained load.

Incredible material if you design around the asterisks.
