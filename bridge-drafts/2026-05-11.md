## LinkedIn Post

Injection molding tolerance is ±0.1mm. Sounds fine until you stack 4 features and suddenly you're 0.4mm off and the part doesn't fit.

Learned this the hard way when a snap fit that worked perfectly in CAD cracked every single sample. The issue wasn't any one dimension—it was the accumulation of small variances across multiple features.

Tolerance stacking will humble you. Design for it or pay for it later.
