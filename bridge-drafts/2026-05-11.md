# May 11, 2026 - Tolerance Stacking

**Category:** MFG_HOT_TAKE

## X Post (~280 char)

your snap fit has 0.3mm clearance in cad. but the hook is ±0.1mm. the catch is ±0.1mm. the wall it mounts to is ±0.1mm. worst case: all three go the wrong direction and your clearance becomes 0mm. it binds. or all three go the other way and you have 0.6mm gap - it doesn't click. that's tolerance stacking. design for the worst case, not the nominal.

## LinkedIn Post

Your snap fit has 0.3mm clearance in CAD. But the hook is ±0.1mm. The catch is ±0.1mm. The wall it mounts to is ±0.1mm.

Worst case: all three go the wrong direction and your clearance becomes 0mm. It binds.

Or all three go the other way and you have 0.6mm gap - it doesn't click.

That's tolerance stacking. Design for the worst case, not the nominal.
