## LinkedIn Post

Tolerance stacking is one of those simple-but-hard concepts in mechanical engineering.

Your snap fit has 0.3mm clearance. Hook ±0.1mm + catch ±0.1mm + wall ±0.1mm = ±0.3mm total variance.

Worst case tight: 0.3 - 0.3 = 0mm, binds.
Worst case loose: 0.3 + 0.3 = 0.6mm, won't click.

Fix: design for worst case, add compliance, or tighten tolerances on the features that matter most.
