## LinkedIn Post

Cork fabric. The "sustainable leather alternative" that looks great in pitch decks.

It's real cork bonded to a fabric backing, usually cotton or polyester. Soft, lightweight, water-resistant.

The catch: it cracks at tight radii. Try a sharp fold and it splits. Also scratches easily and the color fades in sun.

Works for flat panels, not structured goods.
