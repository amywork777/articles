## LinkedIn Post

Every time someone says "vegan leather" I check the spec sheet.

9 out of 10 times it's polyurethane - petroleum-based plastic that doesn't break down. You didn't save a cow. You made plastic.

Real alternatives exist (mycelium, cactus, pineapple leaf) but they're expensive and barely shipping at scale. Read the materials list.
